# Unlocking-Profits

Unlocking Profits: Ranking Product Categories & Customer Segments

📌 Overview

This project analyzes shopping behavior data to identify high‑revenue product categories and customer segments. The goal is to provide actionable insights for marketing, inventory planning, and customer engagement strategies.

📊 Dataset

Source: Shopping behavior dataset

Shape: 3,900 rows × 16 columns

Null values: 0

Duplicates: 0

Key Features:

Category

Gender

Age

Purchase Amount (USD)

Season

Review Rating

Previous Purchases

Subscription

Payment Method

🔍 Analysis

Univariate Analysis

Histogram shows most purchases fall within a mid‑range bracket.

Seasonal distribution is balanced, with Spring showing slightly higher activity.

Bivariate Analysis
Bar graphs and scatter plots highlight relationships between categories and spending.

Heatmap reveals correlations between numerical features (e.g., review ratings and repeat purchases).

💡 Insights

Clothing & Accessories are the top revenue drivers.

Age group 26–55 contributes the highest spending.

Female customers spend slightly more per purchase than males.

Winter & Spring seasons show peak sales activity.

High review ratings correlate with repeat purchases.

🎯 Recommendations

Focus marketing and inventory planning on Clothing & Accessories.

Target the 26–55 age group with tailored campaigns.

Engage female customers through loyalty programs and personalized offers.

Plan inventory around seasonal peaks (Winter & Spring).

📈 Expected Impact

Applying these strategies can unlock an estimated 20–30% profit margin.
